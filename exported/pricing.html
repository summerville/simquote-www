Do I have to sign a long term contract?

No. SimQuote is a pay-as-you-go system so you simply pay month-to-month. There are no long term contracts or commitments on your part. If you cancel, you’ll be billed for the current month, but you won’t be billed again. You can also export all of your data to a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet at any time.

Are there any upfront fees?

No. There are no upfront fees at all to begin using SimQuote. You can sign up now and begin using it today without entering a credit card.

What are my payment options?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and AMEX through our online payment system. If you would like to prepay for 12 months you can pay with an invoice. Please contact us at info@simquote.com.

Do you offer any discounts?

You can prepay for 12 months and receive a 10% discount on your purchase. If you're a very large organization, we sometimes offer discounts on comprehensive bundles of products and services. We believe in giving back, so we have a special program for nonprofits and educational institutions. Please contact us at info@simquote.com.

What web browsers work with Fleetio?

We support Internet Explorer 7 and higher, Firefox 3 and higher, Safari 4 and higher, Chrome 4 and higher.
